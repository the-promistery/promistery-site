Our CSA Program

CSA, or Community Supported Agriculture, originally from Japan in the 1960s, over time has become more common with small local organic minded farms. The idea to build a relationship between the farm and the community it serves. You sign up and become an member of the farm, an active investor in the farm, your dividends a share of the bounty of produce the Promistery produces. Freshly harvested food, first access to harvested produce, a discount on that produce, and the peace of mind knowing where and how your food is grown! The Promistery promises it will use only organic and sustainable farming practices. Every week you receive a heafty share of fresh produce from a variety of vetetables, herbs, fruit, and flowers!

CSA's typically run for a set number of weeks. Our members will receive local organically grown produce for a guaranteed 20 weeks from the week of June 14 to the last week of October. Any weeks before or after then where we have food available will be provided gratis to members! If we have produce ready before June or after November we will let you know and you will get those week for free!

The CSA is a Free Choice CSA, meaning that members are able to choose what is in their share each week. If the memebers do not want to do a free choice CSA they can opt for a tradional CSA where we pick and prepare your share for you. Free choice members will have a choice between three share types: Famly share (173 points), Full share (112 points), and Half Share (53 points). Points are alloted every week to each share type. Members use their points to redeem produce. For example: a head of lettuce is 6 points, a pound of zuchhini is 4 points, a bunch of radish is 7 points, a bunch Kale is 7 points, and a pint of tomatoes is 10 points, add up to 34 points. If you have the Half Share (53 points) you would have 19 points left over. Each point is equivalent to about $.50. If there are a couple of points left but not enout to redeem we will round them up to an item with a close point value.

Members pay for the full 20 weeks before the season starts. Member, subject to the fluctuations inherit therein.

Who We Are

The Promistery is a family owned no-till, organically grown, farm located in St. Catharines, Ontario. We started out in 2020 with hopes of bringing healthy produce to our local community free of pesticides and other harmful fertilizers. We currently offer flowers, herbs and vegetables, and are looking to expand in the future.

CSA Terms

  1. Promistery Pledge: We promise to provide you with the most nutrient dense, delicous, and bountiful produce as possible! With warm hearts we welcome all who can to come visit the Promistery to share in its bounty and beauty. Come for a hike, see the sights! Purchase produce! Say bonjour to some of the animals!
  2. My Share of the Bounty: Much on the list will be available consitently throughout the season, some things are seasonal, and sometimes there are accidents. We promise to provide as much variety at the highest quality as possible!
  3. Acts of God: Sometimes nature gets the best of us and there are crop failures, we will do our best to make up for any losses, we appriciate your understanding!
  4. Refunds: No refunds for the most part (crop loss, halfway through you decide the CSA is not for you, etc.), it will be taken on a case by case basis.
  5. Free Choice Promistery Pickup: If you want to quickly pick up your share and not choose its contents at the Promistery, please email us that week's "My Share Choice" form by midnight the day before your pickup date. If you are picking up Tuesday email us by Monday midnight.
  6. Free Choice Delivey/Pickup Point: Please fill out and email us the that week's "My Share Choice" form by Sunday midnight for your Tuesday delivery/pickup. If we don't recive the "My Share Choice" form by then, It will be the Promistery picks that week.
  7. If you cannot pick up or recieve delivery of your share, please let us know.

    You can:
    1. Have someone else pickup your share for you, please arrange with us.
    2. We can leave your share in the cooler and you can come pick it up within 2 days.
    3. We are happy to donate it!
  8. Swap/Stack/Carry over shares: We don't allow people to share/stack/carry over shares. For example you miss a week and the next week you want to pick up 2 shares. You would only get the share for the current week and not the one you missed.
  9. Delivery: we deliver to many areas in the St. Catharines/Niagara Region. We will let you know if you qualify, shortly after you email us the form.
  10. Apartment Delivery: If you qualify for delivery and live in an apartment please include your phone number and buzzer ( if applicable), or make some arrangments for us to leave it with concierge or something like that. We will wait for a few minutes before moving on.
  11. CSA Payments: in order of preference
    1. E-Transfer:
      • Email: promistery@protonmail.com
      • Question: Anything you want.
      • Password: promistery
    2. Cash:
      We will cordinate with you to receive cash by the first CSA date.
    3. Silver/Gold/Platinum Bullion:
      We will cordinate with you to receive bullion priced by the spot price + $2/oz premium at close on the date that we accept your CSA pledge. Refunds in cash only. Maximum of $100 refund.
      We can pickup bullion from you.
    4. Credit/Debit
      You will be chared a merchant fee of 2.9% of your order total. We will send a Square invoice. To be paid by the first CSA date.
  12. If we have food available in the weeks before June 14 and after the last week of October, CSA memebrs will get those weeks gratis. We will email details when that happens.
  13. THANK YOU SO MUCH! Looking forward to feeding you!

Our CSA Program

CSA, or Community Supported Agriculture, originally from Japan in the 1960s, over time has become more common with small local organic minded farms. The idea to build a relationship between the farm and the community it serves. You sign up and become an member of the farm, an active investor in the farm, your dividends a share of the bounty of produce the Promistery produces. Benefits include: freshly harvested food, first access to harvested produce, a discount on that produce, and the peace of mind knowing where and how your food is grown! The Promistery promises it will use only organic and sustainable farming practices. Every week you receive a hefty share of fresh produce from a variety of vegetables, herbs, fruit, and flowers!

CSA SIGN UP

Our members will receive local organically grown produce for a guaranteed 20 weeks from the week of June 14 to the last week of October. Any weeks before or after then where we have food available will be provided gratis to members! If we have produce ready before June or after November we will let you know and you will get those week for free!

The CSA is a Free Choice CSA, meaning that members are able to choose what is in their share each week. If the memebers do not want to do a free choice CSA they can opt for a tradional CSA where we pick and prepare your share for you.

Points are alloted every week to each share type. Members use their points to redeem produce. For example: a 3 heads of lettuce is 6 points, a pound of zuchhini is 4 points, a bunch of radish is 7 points, a bunch Kale is 7 points, and a pint of tomatoes is 10 points, add up to 46 points. If you have the Half Share (54 points) you would have 8 points left over for other produce. Each point is equivalent to about $.50. A half share is 54 points with a value of $27 at a price of $25 a week for a total price of $500 for the season. Over the whole season that $40 dollars of savings, with the family share the savings are $200! If there are a couple of points left at the end of the week but not enout to redeem an item we will round them up to an item with a close point value.

The Promistery CSA Share Plans:
  1. Family Share - $1500 (173 points/wk @ $75/wk for 20wks) - $1700 value
  2. Full Share - $1000 (112 points/wk @ $50/wk for 20wks) - $1120 value
  3. Half Share - $500 (54 points/wk @ $25/wk for 20wks) - $540 value

CSA pick-ups at the Promistery will be on Tuesdays and Fridays from 3:30pm - 7:00pm. Either come to the Farm Stand to choose your produce there, or order ahead of time for a quicker pick-up. We will send out a weekly "My Share Choice" form, fill it out and email us your choices by midnight the day before your pickup date.

Members can also choose to pickup on Tuesday evenings at a central location in St Catharines (time and location TBD) or opt for delivery. We deliver to many areas in the St. Catharines/Niagara Region. We will let you know if you qualify, shortly after you email us the form. Delivery is $7.50 a week for a total of $150 a season. please include your phone number in the CSA Signup Form if you choose delivery so we can get a hold of you in case we have trouble delivering to you.

Members will recieve weekly newsletters with "My Share Choice" forms to fill out to choose the contents of their shares, updates to the farm, details about farm visits, and recipies to help you incorporate local, healthy, nutrient dense foods into your weekly menu plan.

Members pay for the full 20 weeks in advance before the season starts. Members are, in essence, shareholders in the farm, thus becoming invested in its successes and failures. This joint venture allows the farmer to receive capital before the season starts when they need it most and allows them to be in the field during the growing season instead of worrying about selling their produce. Payment will due after both parties finalize the pledge.

CSA Terms

  1. Promistery Pledge: We promise to provide you with the most nutrient dense, delicous, and bountiful produce as possible! With warm hearts we welcome all who can to come visit the Promistery to share in its bounty and beauty. Come for a hike, see the sights! Purchase produce! Say bonjour to some of the animals!
  2. My Share of the Bounty: Much on the list will be available consitently throughout the season, some things are seasonal, and sometimes there are accidents. We promise to provide as much variety at the highest quality as possible!
  3. Acts of God: Sometimes nature gets the best of us and there are crop failures, we will do our best to make up for any losses, we appriciate your understanding!
  4. Refunds: No refunds for the most part (crop loss, halfway through you decide the CSA is not for you, etc.), it will be taken on a case by case basis.
  5. Free Choice "My Share Choice" form: If for any reason the filled out form is not accurate, for example you don't use all your points that week. In that case we will fill the remaining for you. Same with if there are too many items we will take some out if the point tally is too high. We will try to make the form as easy as possible to avoid this.
  6. Free Choice Promistery Pickup: If you want to quickly pick up your share and not choose its contents at the Promistery, please email us that week's "My Share Choice" form by midnight the day before your pickup date. If you are picking up Tuesday email us by Monday midnight.
  7. Free Choice Delivey/Pickup Point: Please fill out and email us the that week's "My Share Choice" form by Sunday midnight for your Tuesday delivery/pickup. If we don't recive the "My Share Choice" form by then, It will be the Promistery picks that week.
  8. If you cannot pick up or recieve delivery of your share, please let us know.

    You can:
    1. Have someone else pickup your share for you, please arrange with us.
    2. We can leave your share in the cooler and you can come pick it up within 2 days.
    3. We are happy to donate it!
  9. Swap/Stack/Carry over shares: We don't allow people to share/stack/carry over shares. For example you miss a week and the next week you want to pick up 2 shares. You would only get the share for the current week and not the one you missed.
  10. Delivery: we deliver to many areas in the St. Catharines/Niagara Region. We will let you know if you qualify, shortly after you email us the form.
  11. Apartment Delivery: If you qualify for delivery and live in an apartment please include your phone number and buzzer ( if applicable), or make some arrangments for us to leave it with concierge or something like that. We will wait for a few minutes before moving on.
